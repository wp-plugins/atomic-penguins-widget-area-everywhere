==Description==

The Atomic Penguins Widget Area Everywhere is a plugin that lets you create custom widget area and give you the freedom to display it anywhere in your website. May it be a page or above the header, below the footer, or just another sidebar, you can create it with our plugin.

==Installation==

1. Upload plugin.zip to wp/content/plugins directory
2. Activate the plugins through the 'Plugins' menu in Wordpress Admin
3. A new widget option will appear on the widets menu called "Widget Everywhere"
